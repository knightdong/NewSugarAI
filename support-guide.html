User Guide

Learn how to use Sugar.AI effectively

1 Getting Started with Sugar.AI

Welcome to Sugar.AI! This comprehensive guide will help you get the most out of our platform and create amazing AI companions.

Create Your Account

Sign up for a free account to start exploring Sugar.AI. You'll get access to basic features and can upgrade to premium for more advanced capabilities.

Explore Pre-made Characters

Browse our collection of pre-designed AI characters, each with unique personalities and appearances. Click on any character to view details and start generating content.

Purchase Gems

Buy Gems to unlock premium features and generate unlimited content. Different features cost different amounts of Gems.

2 Exploring Characters

Start by browsing our collection of pre-made AI characters. Each character has unique traits and personalities designed to provide different types of companionship and entertainment.

  • Browse the Gallery: Scroll through our character collection to find someone who appeals to you
  • View Character Details: Click on any character to see their full profile, including personality traits and background
  • Generate Content: Use any character to create personalized images and videos
  • Save Favorites: Mark characters you like for easy access later

üí° Pro Tip

Try different characters to discover which personality types you connect with most. Each character offers unique interactions and content generation possibilities.

3 Creating Custom Characters

Use our Create feature to design your own AI companion from scratch. This is where you can truly personalize your experience.

Choose Your Style

Select from Realistic, Anime, or other artistic styles to match your preferences.

Customize Appearance

Select ethnicity, age, hair style, eye color, body type, and outfit to create your perfect companion.

Set Background

Choose a background setting that reflects your character's personality and lifestyle.

  • Style Selection: Choose between Realistic, Anime, or other artistic styles
  • Physical Features: Customize ethnicity, age, hair style, eye color, and body type
  • Fashion Choices: Select outfits that match your character's personality
  • Background Settings: Choose environments that complement your character

4 Generating Content

Once you have a character, you can generate various types of content to bring your AI companion to life.

AI Image Generation

Create static images with various poses, outfits, and scenes. Perfect for profile pictures or artistic content.

AI Video Generation

Generate short videos with different actions and scenes. Bring your character to life with movement and animation.

Group Photo Creation

Create images featuring multiple characters together in various scenarios and settings.

  • Image Generation: Create static images with various poses and outfits (15-25 Gems)
  • Video Generation: Generate short videos with different actions and scenes (35-50 Gems)
  • Group Photos: Create images with multiple characters (varies by complexity)
  • Custom Prompts: Use your own descriptions for more personalized content

üéØ Best Practices

Be specific with your prompts for better results. Include details about poses, outfits, settings, and mood to get exactly what you want.

5 Managing Your Account

Use the Dashboard to manage your account, track your usage, and access support resources.

  • Generation History: View all your created images and videos
  • Gem Balance: Track your remaining Gems and purchase more when needed
  • Account Settings: Update your profile, password, and preferences
  • Support Access: Get help through our comprehensive support system

View History

Access your complete generation history to review, download, or share your favorite creations.

Manage Gems

Check your Gem balance and purchase more credits to continue creating content.

Get Support

Access our help resources, contact support, or report issues directly from the dashboard.

6 Gem System & Pricing

Gems are our platform currency used for generating content. Understanding the pricing helps you plan your usage effectively.

  • Image Generation: 15-25 Gems per image (depending on complexity)
  • Video Generation: 35-50 Gems per video (depending on length and quality)
  • Character Creation: 50 Gems for custom character design
  • Premium Features: Varies by feature and subscription level

üí∞ Cost-Saving Tips

Purchase Gems in larger bundles for better value. Premium members get discounts on all features and access to exclusive content.

7 Tips for Best Results

Follow these tips to get the most out of Sugar.AI and create amazing content.

  • Be Specific: Include detailed descriptions in your prompts for better results
  • Experiment: Try different poses, outfits, and scenes to discover new possibilities
  • Save Favorites: Keep track of your best generations for future reference
  • Use Tags: Organize your content with tags for easy searching
  • Share Responsibly: Only share content that you're comfortable with others seeing

Prompt Writing

Write detailed, specific prompts that include pose, outfit, setting, lighting, and mood for best results.

Content Organization

Use tags and folders to organize your generated content for easy access and management.

Quality Settings

Choose higher quality settings for important content and standard settings for experimentation.

8 Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, here are common solutions and how to get help.

  • Content Not Generating: Check your Gem balance and try again
  • Slow Loading: Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Account Issues: Contact support through the dashboard
  • Payment Problems: Check your payment method and try again
